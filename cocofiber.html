Coco Fiber/Coir

Coconut coir is a natural fiber extracted from the outer husk of coconut and used in products such as floor mats, doormats, brushes and mattresses. Coir is the fibrous material found between the hard, internal shell and the outer coat of a coconut.

Specification: Dried clean, golden brown, bright.

  • Moisture: Max 18% (+-2%).
  • Impurities and dust: Max 3 %.
  • Volume: 170cm x 70cm x 40cm.
  • Length of fiber string: 5-25cm (80% 20cm+).

    Packing and Shipping: In bales each weighing 100-120 kg, tightened with plastic straps:
    • 20 feet Container: Max load 11-12 mt.
    • 40 feet Container: Max load 16 -17 mt.
