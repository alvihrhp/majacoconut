Maja Coconut is a company that is committed to providing the best quality of coconut derivatives. In order to further solidify its market position, our company has been collaborating with local farmers in Indonesia who cultivate fresh coconuts. We export our products worldwide for the international market, whether you are a one time buyer or want to buy in bulk, we will happily serve you. Noticing that there is a massive demand for coconut derivatives and knowing well that the country we live in produces the best quality of coconut products, we as a company intend to supply different areas of the world with the coconut we have. Having a wide knowledge in the industry, we believe that our company is able to supply your demanded product, ranging from Coconut Briquette Charcoal to Coconut Fiber.

Vision: Our vision is to be the preferred trademark and supplier of quality coconut derivatives worldwide.

Mission: We have established a few missions to be able to tackle our vision;

  • Create customer satisfaction through delivering services and products that exceed customer expectations
  • Establish our trademark through innovative marketing.
  • Ensure the utilisation of high-quality and cost-effective raw materials.
  • Wanting to always learn more about the current and future market of the coconut industry and how to perform better.
