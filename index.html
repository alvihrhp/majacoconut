A TASTE OF INDONESIA'S COCONUTS

Coconut is a gift for tropical countries like Indonesia, all parts of the plant are useful for daily life, from the leaves, the fruits, and even the trunk. It has been observed that Indonesia is the biggest producer of coconut in the world, with plantations covering an area of around 3.88 million ha in total. It is our duty to deliver our coconuts to your home country.
